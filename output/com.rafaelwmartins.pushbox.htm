说明
The objective is to put all the boxes in the marked positions by pushing one at a time. Earn stars by completing levels with fewer pushes. Leaderboard and Achievements powered by Google Play Games!

Push Box features 240 levels, 90 of them free. It is possible to skip the levels that you are unable to complete, or you can learn by seeing their solutions. Push Box supports tablets and HD screens. There are no ads!

This game is the successor of Lady Bug Sokoban, from the same creator.
