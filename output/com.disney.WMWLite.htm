To use Disney Fantasy Navigator, download the app, and once on board, connect to the ship’s WiFi network—complimentary to app users only—and enjoy your cruise in an exciting new way.

With the official cruise app Disney Fantasy Navigator, you’ll know everything that’s happening on the ship across your entire cruise. Explore the Disney Fantasy from bow to stern. Learn about the ports you’ll be visiting and check the weather for the day. With Disney Fantasy Navigator you can carry the entire cruise schedule with you and never miss a thing.

Disney Fantasy Navigator is only the first leg of our journey with the new Disney Cruise Line on board app. Soon the app will be available for Disney Dream, Disney Magic and Disney Wonder and we will be adding new features along the way.

Embark on your Disney cruise with Disney Fantasy Navigator!

PREPARE FOR YOUR CRUISE DAY
• Know what’s happening every day, from shows to shopping
• Review your ports of call and at-sea days
• Get daily special spa and shopping offers and never miss a deal

GET THE INFO YOU NEED TO HELP YOU PLAN
• View all on board activities across your entire voyage
• Know what’s happening next and where to go
• Read details about activities you’re interested in
• Find Disney Characters on board
• Save favorite activities in one convenient list

NAVIGATE THE SHIP
• Explore your ship deck by deck, from bow to stern
• Find locations of the activities you want to do

Disney Fantasy Navigator helps you make daily plans, navigate the ship and enjoy every cruise day to its fullest. Download the app to get started!
