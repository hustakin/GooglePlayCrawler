A fully colored, Android “Ice Cream Sandwich” OS inspired 3D theme for phones and tablets that include the T-mobile/CyanogenMod7 theme engine. Ics3D was built and tested on MDPI and HDPI devices by Team Syndicate, the creators of Honeycomb 3D, HCL Launcher, and ICS Launcher.
