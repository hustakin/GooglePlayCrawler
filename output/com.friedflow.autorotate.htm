Auto-Rotate Status Bar Switch toggles autorotate on/off.

The switch is placed in the status bar for easy access from other applications. You can also use it as a widget-like shortcut on your home screen.

Note: The app only toggles the auto-rotate setting that is found in Android's settings. It does not lock the orientation to landscape mode.

Stop the display auto-rotating to the right or left side , without affecting automatic rotation (landscape mode) to the other side.

Since Android 2.2 (Froyo) the display is enabled to autorotate in both directions without any option to disable the autorotation to the right or the left side. It for right-handed persons does not make sense to keep the rotation to the right side active. The opposite is true for left-handed persons.

The switching off of the autorotation to one side, which is controlled automatically by this app, on the other hand allows usage or the phone when lying in bed on the autorotation-disabled side without having the screen turning.
