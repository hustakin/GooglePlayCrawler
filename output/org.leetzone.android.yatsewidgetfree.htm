Full featured XBMC remote with widgets and streaming capabilities

Yatse is the most complete and stable XBMC remote available in Play Store and the only one with a 4.9 rating.

Take full control of your XBMC with complete library support, beautiful widgets, and streaming functions optimized for phones and tablets.

Major functions:
◆ Voice Commands : Give orders in natural language sentences
◆ Offline library synchronization : List your XBMC medias everywhere
◆ Modern and intuitive Holo interface
◆ Support Multiple XMBC installation (And centralized Mysql database)
◆ XBMC Starter for Windows : Start XBMC directly from your remote
◆ Multiple Widgets included for fast remote actions, now playing details or list recent medias
◆ Optimized for phones and tablets. Multiple configurable views to fit your needs
◆ Full support for Media Player Classic (MPC or MPC-HC) as an external player
◆ Cloud Save to keep your hosts and settings across devices
◆ Pvr support
◆ Stream your videos to your Android devices or UPnP / AirPlay device
◆ Send your Android medias to your Xbmc or UPnP / AirPlay device
◆ Completely configurable to make Yatse fill all your needs

Features:
* DashClock extension
* Support all XBMC medias (Movies / Movie Sets / Music / Tv Shows / Music Videos / Pictures)
* Support for playlists and file listing (XBMC Sources / Addons)
* Support all XBMC remote commands from an optimized views or widgets
* Support Android notifications for currently playing medias
* Support Android Lockscreen controls / widgets (ICS / JB and up)
* Quickly find your medias with complete search (Includes global search)
* Sort your media as you need
* Multiple beautiful display modes for media listing
* Toggle watched status of your medias
* Detailed now playing views with direct selection of Subtitles or Audio streams
* Support Android from 2.2 (Froyo) to 4.3 (Jelly Bean)
* Wake on Lan (WOL) support (Long press the media center name in any screen)
* Change your current Wallpaper to the currently playing media Fanart or Thumbnail
* Multiple plugins for SMS / Call, Android notifications, ...
* Send media to XBMC from YouTube or browser. (Support XBMC YouTube / Justin.tv / MyVideo.de / Dailymotion / ...)
* IMDB button when available to get more details on medias
* Support Tv Shows posters and banners
* Support resuming of medias
* Optimized for speed even on older phones
* Optimized for very low battery usage
* Support themes with the unlocker
* Api to control Xbmc / Yatse from other Android application
* Translated in multiple languages (EN / FR / ES / DE / DA / PT_PT / PT_BR / IT / RU / HE / NL / PL / TR / KR / LT / FI / CA)
* Contact me if you want to help translating to your language

And much more, just install and try.
