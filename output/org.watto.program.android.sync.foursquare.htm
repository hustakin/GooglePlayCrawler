说明
Adds your Foursquare friends into your Contacts address book, and re-syncs them when their details change.

Syncs all of your friends contact information including...
* Name and Photo
* Phone number and email address
* Facebook and Twitter accounts
* Home City and Last Check-in Location (including Shout-out message)
* Gender and User ID

To use this application, go to your "Settings" menu and choose the "Accounts and Sync" option. You can then "Add" a new account, and Foursquare should appear in the list.

To change how often your Foursquare contacts are updated from the server, go to your "Settings" menu and choose the "Accounts and Sync" option. Choose the "Foursquare" account, then choose the "Account Settings" option.

Google have started disabling sync access from any apps that are not explicitly created by Google.
