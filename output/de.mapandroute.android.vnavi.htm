V-Navi - The full-featured navigation app for Android that brings you quicker to your goal! Works in conjuction with various apps (e.g., "Gelbe Seiten," "Das Telefonbuch,", among others), even free of charge, as conditions may apply.

ATTENTION: Navigation can be started in Germany, Austria, Switzerland and optional in Western Europe.

NEW, traffic data. With the high quality traffic you will get a complete overview of the market. Traffic congestion traffic congestion avoidance. The V-Navi: you will thus always the best route. The traffic density is displayed in red, yellow, green in the map. Germany, Annual, 30 days free of charge with no obligation to buy. Internet connection is required.

The new version of V-Navi works with offboard maps (with limited functionality) to enable immediate navigation after download. The map data for Germany, Austria and Switzerland is downloaded once at the start of the first use of V-Navi (ca. 300 MB). Thereafter, an Internet connection is only necessary for a short time at app-startup.
