A: CardioTrainer is an app focused on exercise and activity whereas Noom Weight Loss Coach is an all-in-one solution for those looking to lose weight, eat healthier, get fit, and stay motivated. Q: Why does CardioTrainer want access to my contacts and my Google account?

FAQs

Q: Where does nutrition and exercise science for the app come from?
A: We have a dedicated team of dieticians, exercise scientists, personal trainers, and behavior experts that program their knowledge into the Noom Weight Loss Coach.
Q: What if I already use CardioTrainer or Calorific?
A: If you already use CardioTrainer, Noom will NOT erase your data, and you can transfer it using your permanent account. Noom will not share data from CardioTrainer or Calorific, so if you want to record new tracks or food, be sure to do so from Noom.
Q: Why does Noom want access to my Google account and contacts? What about other permissions?
A: Your Google account is used only to create a permanent account so you can backup and recover your data. The contacts are only used IF you want to connect with friends who also use Noom to track their activity — it’s not used otherwise. This information is not stored anywhere, nor will it ever be used for any other purpose. More information about the permissions requested by this application: http://goo.gl/lGnJ4
