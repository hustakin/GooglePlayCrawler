Take a picture or use an existing image & trace over it before removing the picture to create magical art! Don't tell your friends there was an original picture, and watch their reaction when they "discover" you artist talent!

Start with an existing image on your Samsung Galaxy Note, or take a picture of a person or a scene. Then easily trace over it. If your picture is too dark, use the volume buttons to adjust the brightness. To see how the sketch is coming along, you can remove the original by shaking the phone. Shake again and the picture will be back so you continue your work. Using two fingers you can zoom in to work on tiny details. Change the pen, color, or width as needed. Making mistakes are not costly as you can undo/redo or just simply use the adjustable eraser. Once done, remove the original picture and you will end up with a breathtaking sketch! Save your creation on your Samsung Galaxy Note or share it with your friends through social networks, by email, or via Bluetooth, all from inside the app.

With "Traces for Samsung Galaxy Note" one can look like the next Picasso! Create astonishing caricatures and artworks that are clearly hand drawn and not computer generated. Create avatars, draw a preview of a remodeling project, or ... The possibilities are endless and only limited by your imagination.
