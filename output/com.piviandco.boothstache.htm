PiVi & Co is launching the Android version of its new app BoothStache (by the creators of AgingBooth, FatBooth, BaldBooth, MixBooth & UglyBooth). BoothStache for Android is available to download for free on the Android Market.

What would you look like with a mustache? And what about your family, friends or colleagues? Find out with BoothStache, an easy to use and funny way to add different types of mustaches on face photos, instantly on your Android device. For a more realistic result, BoothStache automatically adjusts color and brightness of the 7 different mustaches already available (more to come).

Ready to let it grow?

Tip
• Drag mustache for a more precise adjustment

Features
• Works with photos taken with your Android device camera or from your photo gallery
• Auto-cropping using face detection
• Automatically adjusts the color of the mustache for a more realistic result
• Transformation process is instant with no internet connection needed
• Shake device to see before and after views
• Scroll results into the app gallery
• Save results to your photo gallery
• Share with your friends via email, Facebook or Twitter

Warning:
• BoothStache is a funny application made for entertainment purposes only.
• BoothStache works best with front face photo and uniform lighting.

BoothStache for Android is optimised for Android platform v2.1 or later. In order to help improve this version, should you have any trouble using it, please feel free to send your feedback at androidfeedback@piviandco.com.
