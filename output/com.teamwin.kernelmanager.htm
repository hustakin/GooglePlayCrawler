BootFlash Pro lets you choose an image stored on your phone to install as your rooted phone's splash screen. Unlike The free version of BootFlash, BootFlash Pro does not overlay a watermark on your image. The splash screen is the image that is displayed on your screen when you turn on the phone before the boot animation begins to play. The splash screen installed by BootFlash Pro will stick across different ROMs, so that you can always retain the splash screen that you enjoy. You can change your splash screen as often as you want.
BootFlash Pro currently works on:
HTC Inspire 4G
HTC Desire HD
HTC Vision
HTC ThunderBolt
HTC G2
HTC Desire Z
HTC Droid Incredible
HTC Evo 4G
HTC Evo 3D
HTC MyTouch 4G (not Slide)
and more to come!

Please do not attempt to use BootFlash Pro on any phone not listed here.

Instructions:
If you are using an Inspire, Desire HD, ThunderBolt, G2, Vision, Desire Z, Evo 3D, MyTouch 4G or Sensation, just choose an image within the app and your splash screen will be installed. If on another device, after you choose an image your phone will be rebooted into the Bootloader. Once there, press the lock button to choose Bootloader. When prompted to install an update (your splash screen) press the Volume Up button to install. When you reboot your splash screen will be installed!

We have had multiple occurrences of people downloading, using and then returning BootFlash Pro. Due to these unfortunate occurrences, BootFlash Pro will now revert to your original splash screen once you uninstall the app.

Note: BootFlash uses approximately 1 Megabyte of data each time you install a custom splash screen.
