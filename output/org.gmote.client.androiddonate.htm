PicLearn helps you learn Mandarin Chinese in a fun and visual way. Learn new words with pictures and sounds, and perfect your pronunciation through the use of voice recognition.
With PicLearn, you can learn and review words on the go and build up your Chinese vocabulary.

You can select between 3 modes:
* PicIt presents you with a Mandarin Chinese word and 4 images. Tap the image that represents the word.

* SayIt uses voice recognition to help you practice your prononciation. Tap the microphone picture and say the word that the image represents.

* CueIt shows you cue cards which you can use to quickly learn a new set of words.
