说明
'AlSalah', pronounced as AsSalah, calculates the (5) Salah timings (Salat, Salah, Namaz, Solat, Prayer in Islam, Prayer Times Calculator). Original app was written in 2001 for daily use and traveling purposes. MashaAllah now available on multitude of devices, including phones and tablets.

AlSalah app is really an efficient, fast and light-weight application (uses least amount of cpu or battery power and is less than 300KB)

Features:

✔ Hijri Islamic date
✔ Qibla compass (Direction/Distance to Ka'bah from True North)
✔ GPS and Network based Location
✔ Arabic (Andriod 4+), Urdu (Andriod 4+), English, Español. (Please volunteer for translation to your language)
✔ Places - Save your favorite places
✔ History - While traveling keep a trail
✔ Change Date option
✔ 4 Imams option
✔ 12/24 Hour option
✔ Hijri Adjustment option
✔ Time difference from next day's Fajr, Shurooq & Maghrib (Can be turned off in Settings)
✔ Swipe gestures right/left/up/down to change date
✔ Double tap to goto today's date
✔ Highlighting current prayer. (Forbidden prayer times show in RED)
✔ Arabic, Urdu, English, Spanish and Turkish languages

Optimized for Mobile Phones, Tablets, Nook and Fire.

www.alsajdah.com

Coming Soon (inshaAllah):
Alarms in next release.

Notes:
• High latitude problem is planned to be solved next summer inshaAllah.
• This app is not intended to have alarms or notifications so that is my lowest priority if it will ever happen.
• This app only uses 18 degrees formula for Fajr and Isha.
• Please report bugs.
• Please volunteer for translations to more languages.
• Always for Free, inshaAllah.
