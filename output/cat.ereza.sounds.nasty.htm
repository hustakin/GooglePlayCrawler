Next bus Barcelona allows you to check how much time it will take for the bus to reach your bus stop, for all bus stops in Barcelona and surroundings (including night buses ("Nit Bus"), TMB buses and other operators' buses).

You just need to enter the bus stop code (you'll see it at the bus stop) and you'll be able to check how much time is left for all the lines that reach your bus stop, and their routes.

If you don't know the bus stop code, you can also use any of the following options:
* Open nearest bus stop
* Show a map with all bus stops nearby
* Search stops by name, city, line and operator company.
* Scan the QR code from the bus stop

In addition, you'll be able to save the bus stops in a favorites list, so next time you'll just have to push a button to know how much time it will take for the bus to reach your stop, or you can use the widget so it is even easier!

This service works for all TMB and AMB stops, so it includes all the following service operators: Authosa, Baixbus (Mohn, Oliveras, Rosanbus), SGMT, Soler i Sauret, TCC, TMB and TUSGSAL. This includes bus stops from the following towns in the Metropolitan Area: Barcelona, Badalona, L'Hospitalet de Llobregat, Castelldefels, Cornellà de Llobregat, El Prat de Llobregat, Esplugues de Llobregat, Gavà, Montcada i Reixac, Montgat, Sant Adrià de Besòs, Sant Boi de Llobregat, Sant Feliu de Llobregat, Sant Joan Despí, Sant Just Desvern, Santa Coloma de Cervelló, Santa Coloma de Gramenet, Tiana and Viladecans. It is similar to the AMBtempsbus and iBus systems, but it is more complete.

The creators of this app have no relationship with these companies. Data is obtained from these operators, and may be inaccurate.

Permissions explanation:
* Access fine location: locating nearest bus stop and your location on the map.
* Modify SD card/USB storage contents: caching parts of the map.
* Read Google services settings: displaying the Google Maps maps
* Full Internet access and read network state: get data from the servers
