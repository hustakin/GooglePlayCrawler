说明
Location Sound Profile for your phone

Have different sound profiles. Edit them, delete them or add new ones.

Android 4 (Ice Cream Sandwich ICS or Jelly Bean) ties your notification and call ringtone volume together. Now you can solve it. This application will split them.

If the app doesn't open in your device, please, let me know corcanoe@gmail.com

They can be activated with a time limit so you will never forget your phone again in "silent mode", "meeting mode" or "airplane mode".

It works as Nokia, Blackberry or Ericsson old sound managers.

This application also silences your phone while it is rebooting (shutting down and booting again) so you will not hear annoying intro melodies from your operator (silent boot and shut down).

Use different wallpapers or background colors to quickly find your mode or profile.

Set to meeting (or other mode or profile) just for 10 minutes (or whatever time).

Just one beep tone available.

For each profile you may personalize ring tone volume, notifications volume, ringtone melody, notification melody, multimedia/system volume, alarms volume (Android 4), vibration, airplane mode, bluetooth, wifi, data, screen brightness, screen timeout, wallpaper picture or background solid color...

Scheduler feature to automatically change profiles at given times (for example, activate Night at 22:00, Normal at 08:00, etc).

Block list feature:
Select in each profile which calls are going to be allowed and which are going to be rejected. You can automatically send a SMS Text message back, too. Never get awake in the middle of the night for an unwanted call, but be always ready for your kids, wife or husband.
Other uses: reject hidden numbers; reject unknown numbers; the use of wildcards '*' is allowed; very flexible; block my ex-wife/girlfriend, ex-boyfriend/husband calls; Always accept my son's school in meetings...
Please notice that sending Text SMS can cost you money (depending on your rates).

Lock your side volume rocker buttons

Location Tasks:
-Activate profile "Car" when "my car" bluetooth is connected
-Activate profile "Home" when "my home WIFI" is detected
-Activate Wifi when getting close to your neighborhood
-Activate profile Job when getting close to your job

Please, send me any suggestions to corcanoe@gmail.com and I will answer you.
