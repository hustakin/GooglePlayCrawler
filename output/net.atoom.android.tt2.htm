This is a lightweight super fast text based browser for the Dutch NOS Teletekst. It is not the official app published by NOS themselves. It is open source so please visit the homepage for more info and feedback.

Features:
* Very fast and efficient
* References in pages are clickable
* Navigation through gestures
* Configurable personal homepage
* Pagenumber selection through keypad
* Auto refreshes pages
