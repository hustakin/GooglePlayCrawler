说明
Adaptxt is the best and most customizable keyboard in the Android market. It provides word suggestions before you even start typing and learns from your writing style to get smarter and more efficient as you use it.
#1 Android keyboard for language support with 81 dictionaries, Adaptxt is the perfect keyboard application for your Android device.
“Adaptxt is one of the best options for a free and highly functional alternate keyboard. Its error correction and next word prediction capabilities are among the best we have seen”
- Economic Times

# # # #

Key Features:

• Intelligent prediction and error correction in 81 languages; up to 5 dictionaries can be enabled at once.
• 3 different auto correction modes to suit your typing style for the most accurate text entry.
• 39 industry-specific dictionary add-ons covering domains such as business, medicine, law, finance and IT & Telecom.
• Keyboard Customization to create and save your own themes, features to swap keys, modify the key shape, font style, background picture, colors and height of your keyboard. Keyboard Customization can be purchased from the Adaptxt Store.
• Automatic Text Replacement (ATR) feature to create custom shortcuts.
• Navigation keys
• Split keyboard layout for two-thumb typing.
• Learn from your Facebook and Twitter accounts to allow Adaptxt to adapt to your style and vocabulary.
• Share Key to quickly post a message on your social networks.
• A range of vibrant festive and event based themes available for purchase from the Adaptxt Store.

Adaptxt是安卓市場裡最讚的鍵盤軟體，擁有最豐富的鍵盤客製化功能。在你還沒開始輸入之前，Adaptxt便預先提供了字詞選項。您一邊使用Adaptxt的同時，它更學習您的書寫風格，變得更聰明、更有效率。

身為首屈一指的安卓系統鍵盤軟體，Adaptxt支援81組不同語言詞庫，是您安卓手機文字輸入軟體之首選。


- 經濟時報指出：「如果要找個既免費又多功能的輸入軟體，Adaptxt是您最好的選擇。它的糾錯與詞彙預想功能是我們所見過最好的其中之一。」
# # # #
Key Features主要特色:
• 擁有81種語言詞庫；您最多可同時開啟5組詞庫。
• 提供精準的字詞輸入功能和3 種不同的自動糾錯模式。您可依自己的打字習慣選擇最合適的糾錯模式。
• 擁有39 組產業相關專業詞庫(外掛)，包括：商業、醫學、法律、財經、資訊與電信。
• 提供鍵盤客製化功能，讓您可自訂鍵盤主題，交換按鍵位置，變更按鍵的形狀與字型，你也可選擇鍵盤背景圖案、顏色和高度。您可在Adaptxt Store商店加購鍵盤客製化功能
• 提供縮寫擴展功能(ATR)：您可為冗長的句子制定縮寫代碼，輸入代碼時可擴長成長句。
• 剪貼簿功能：選擇、剪下、貼上文字；也可於文字格內移動游標。
• Adaptxt可從臉書和推特學習您的文字風格與新詞。
• 可透過鍵盤上的「分享鍵」快速地在社交網站上貼文。
• 提供電話式鍵盤(12 鍵) 。
• 提供一系列鮮明的鍵盤主題。您可在Adaptxt Store商店加購此項功能。
• Glide Beta – 手指頭直接在螢幕鍵盤上字母間滑動，即可輸入字母。
