说明
It is a simple widget that displays the moon phase and age of the moon.

Function:
- Full moon and new moon, first quarter, last quarter will be announced in the widget.
- The calendar opens the widget when touching.
(The number under each date is the age of the moon of 12:00 a.m. of the day.)
- The calendar is moved by a right or left flick or the arrow button.
- Notify full moon and new moon, first quarter, last quarter in the status bar.
(Notify in a timing of the update of the widget.)

《 How to resize widget (Android 4.0 over) 》
Please set to [ Show(Center) ] or [ Hide ] in settings of [ Display age of the moon ].
Long press of widget ⇒ Release ⇒ To change the size by dragging the dot when four dot and frame of light blue appears.

Notice:
* Please do not install to SD card, even if available in Android settings.

* Since change of the background of a widget may not be reflected at once, please repeat operation 2 or 3 times.

* Android 4.0 or later resizable widgets. (recommend to 2*2)

* If the image of the moon phase does not appear in the center well during resize,
Once deciding on the size of the desired and set to [ None ] or [ Full ] in settings of [ Background of Widget ] .
Finally back to its original setting.

Moon Phase Widget displays the current phase of the moon in a small 2x2 widget. Along with an icon, it will show the name of the phase and what percentage is visible. This is calculated real time and updates itself hourly.

Also, the widget will display the moon rise and set times for your current location, if your device has location information available. To conserve battery, it does not actively use GPS.

Other features:
If you tap on the widget, the current month will be displayed showing the phase of the moon at midnight. You may swipe left or right on the month at a glance, and it will change to the next or previous months. This is a continuous calendar.

Limitations:
Due to wobble and accuracy limitations, the rise and set times may be up to 20 mins off.
