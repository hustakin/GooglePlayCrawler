NOTE: To Galaxy S/S2/S3/S4, Galaxy Note 1/2, Galaxy Nexus, HTC Rezound, and possibly Nexus 4 owners: Please Audio Glow Music Visualizer first. The visualizer will sporadically show a flatline with many music players, due to an Android bug related to the built-in DSP in these phones. This is a problem for all music visualizer apps on these phones, as evidenced by user reviews of other apps. Still looking for a work-around, but we'll probably have to wait for a future version of Android to fix it. If you have this problem, I apologize! Please email me and I will refund you.

Please send me an email about which audio player you use if you aren't seeing meta data. I need to manually implement each player. A player can only be supported if its developer has decided to broadcast meta data within Android and I am able to contact them. I have attempted to contact the makers of Spotify, iHeartRadio, DoubleTwist, and TuneIn. If they get back to me, I might be able to support them.

• Any media players that support Simple Last.fm Scrobbler or ScrobbleDroid.
