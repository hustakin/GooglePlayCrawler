Widgetize my Instagram Photos!

Winstagram enables you to put your favorite instagram photos on your home screen. You can surf your favorite instagram pages and add your favorite photos on your home screen whenever you want.

You can navigate through statigram and get statistics & analysis of your photos. You can also take a snapshot of your statistical information (your followers, your likes, love rate..) and share it through that webpage. Statigram can help you increase your photos' popularity by providing you a good insight on what tags have the most popularity as well.

Download Winstagram and have fun!

Please see the video below before using the app..

What is new (Update 3.5):

You will really like Winstagram's 3.5 version update.

1. Now you can add up to 4 different images as widget to your homescreen!

2. The apps minimum size decreased to 110dp X 110dp (2x2) in order to enable smaller devices to use the widget. You can still resize/enlarge the widget on your home screen to maximum.

3. We included external links to Webstagram and Statigram websites. These websites will provide you with some additional functions and much useful information to help you increase popularity of your photos.

4. We added a new function to retrieve photos from these webpages (Menu->View Image) in full size and open it in a new browser (Menu->Open in New Browser). By opening photos in new browser you will be able to download or set this photos as wallpaper.

5. We added cut/crop buttons for the upper part of any webpage/image you are browsing in addition to the previous cut/crop buttons for the lower part of image.

6. Please note that you can surf through the pictures of who you follow in instagram by this app; however, you will still need the official instagram app to sign up the first time..

User's Guide to Winstagram:

Winstagram enables you to view your Instagram Photos via:
1.Instagram
2.Statigram
3.Webstagram

websites. Just click on the icon of website you want to login.

How to Add Photo's to HomeScreen Widgets:

1. Go to Widgets from your homescreen, choose Winsta1, Winsta2, Winsta3, or Winsta4, and drop it on your homescreen. Then you will see the widgets on your homescreen showing just Winstagram logo for now. If you click on it now, nothing will change yet unless you add photo to widgets by following the steps below. You can add all the 4 widgets together at the same time to your home screen.

2. Open Winstagram app

3. Login to Instagram, Statigram, or Webstagram

4. Go to the photo's page you want to add to homescreen widget.
If you want to retrieve the photo and see it full size, click Menu->View Image
You can crop the picture using the four buttons right to menu.

YOU SHOULD FIRST PUSH ONE OF THE CROP BUTTONS (TO THE RIGHT OF THE "Menu" BUTTON) BEFORE YOU PUSH "Set Page/Img to Widget" BUTTONS

5. While on the photo page, click Menu->Set Page/Img to Widget1
(or Set Page/Img to Widget2, Set Page/Img to Widget3, Set Page/Img to Widget4 for adding images to relative widgets)

6. Click on the widget on your homescreen and the photo you added to your widget will appear on your homescreen now.

What do Menu buttons do:

Home Page : Navigates to opening page
Set Page/Img to Widget1 : Adds photo to Winsta1
Set Page/Img to Widget2 : Adds photo to Winsta2
Set Page/Img to Widget3 : Adds photo to Winsta3
Set Page/Img to Widget4 : Adds photo to Winsta4
View Image : Retrieves a photo and shows it full size. You will see just the photo when you click that button. This button appears only if you are at the photo's page on Instagram or Webstagram.
Four buttons right to menu button : They are used for taking snapshot of the webpage you are currently viewing and cropping the snapshot or photo. After finishing cropping, you should push on one of the Set Page/Img to Widget buttons.
