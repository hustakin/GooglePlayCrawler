Your Tasks, Your Way

Tasks To Do is an easy to use, full-featured task list app. Use it as a checklist, in a GTD style or however you want.

A trial version is available at http://www.refined-apps.com

For questions, remarks, suggestions or feedback mail us at support@refined-apps.com

✓ Current design style

✓ Translations to English, German, French, Spanish, Japanese, Polish, Hungarian, Russian, Chinese

Task fields
✓ Title

✓ 5 Priority levels
