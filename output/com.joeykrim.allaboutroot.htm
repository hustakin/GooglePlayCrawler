so that each move should get a new finished word. Each of the next letter
in the word is separated from the previous by one cell horizontally or
vertically.

1. Before the game start program puts a random word(nominal, nominative,
singular) on a square field with cells. The goal is to fill the entire
field of characters. The player who records the longer words will get
a larger number of points.

2. New words are obtained by adding just one letter in any neighboring cell
of any recorded letters. For each letter of the newly formed word you will
get a one point.

3. Using all available letters is not required but the letters chain which
makes the word must be indivisible and disjoint. The same cell cannot be
used twice in one move.
