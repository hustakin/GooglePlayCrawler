You can specify the number of shortcuts that will be placed next to each other and you can specify the number of rows that will be displayed inside your widget. The first widget you add contains the shortcuts to your most popular apps, the second will contain the less popular ones etc.

So you can have only the most frequently used apps from a predefined subset in a widget on your homescreen.
