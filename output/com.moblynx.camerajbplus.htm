--- FAQ ---

● How can I view the latest photos/videos taken?

Slide the viewfinder from right to left to quickly access the gallery/filmstrip view. You can quickly delete photos you do not like by dragging the unwanted picture up or down.

● Where can I change the camera settings?

You can access this mode by pressing the button on the bottom right or also with a long press on the camera viewfinder

● Why have you taken so long to upgrade to last Jelly Bean 4.3 camera version?

This new version has been completely redesigned from the previous version. We have worked hard to ensure compatibility with most Android devices.

● Why is not Photo Sphere included within the app?

Unfortunately this part of the code is not open source and can not be included within the application.

● I'm not happy with the app and I want a refund.

Of course, we are working hard to add new features and improve compatibility.
