User friendly advanced ebook reader for PDF and EPUB 2.
Read books on your tablet or phone, highlight text, create text annotations, search words in remote dictionaries.

Includes advanced functions for reading PDF files, such as automatic and manual margin removal for PDFs, adjustment to one column for multi-column documents, zooming and shifting (Pan & Zoom),...
Supports Adobe DRM.

Supports the OPDS catalogs format. You can directly browse preset famous catalogs, such as Feedbooks or the Internet Archive or add your own catalogs.

Subscribe to Mantano Cloud (http://cloud.mantano.com, requires a separate paid subscription) and:
- Switch between your phone and tablet and always find your books, annotations, notes, reading positions along with you
- Transform your books and documents into digital reading circles by sharing annotations and learn from others' experience.

Optimized for Honeycomb, and compatible with smartphones.
