Find the cheapest fares fast with the idealo flight app

The flights.idealo.co.uk flight app will help you find cheap flights quickly and easily. Whether you are looking for a low cost flight or scheduled flight, idealo will search offers of more than 900 airlines, over 70,000 routes and a large number of travel agents. Within seconds, you can compare prices for your chosen route and book the cheapest flight. Even before the search begins, the app will display an approximate price based on former searches.

idealo flight app features:

- Find the best date: even before your search begins, the app will display approximate prices based on former searches.

- Nearby departure airports option: the app will automatically show you the nearest alternative airports.

- Airport suggest: our airport database will suggest departure and arrival airports as you make your entry.

- Search history: find the cheapest prices for your favourite routes with only one tap.

- Filters: filter results by number of stopovers, departure and arrival times, price, airline and shop.

- Quick filter: filter results by time, number of stops and airline with a single tap.

- Multiple Destinations: select up to four airports for your journey.

- Share function: send flight offers via email.

- Online booking: book online directly with the airline or travel agent.
