说明
DroidPapers gives an overview of wallpapers and ringtones from official stock Android and famous companies and ROMs of Android. Download the wallpaper or ringtone you like and set it from within the app. App is and stays ad-free.
----
Features
* Open wallpapers in browser.
* Fullscreen view of wallpapers.
* Set wallpaper and ringtones from within the app.
* Set a wallpaper in grayscale or sepia color-scheme.
* Favorite systems to organize your favorite wallpapers and ringtones.
* Download wallpapers and ringtones directly, without setting it as default.
* Play ringtones in the app.
* Share the wallpapers and ringtones you want to share with the world.
* Setup profiles with wallpapers and ringtones. You can activate a profile and all content included gets set as system defaults.
* AutoChanger service to random change your wallpaper based on an interval.
* ShakeChanger service to change your wallpaper to a random one with a shake.
