Face2Gif is the fastest way to create your own animated gifs right from your phone or tablet! It's perfect for reaction gifs (reaction faces, selfies), and time-lapse shots.

This app uses your front or back camera to record your face, and converts it into an animated gif picture to share with your friends, on your blog, and just everywhere!

Please note that if you are sending your pictures via SMS, the receiver have to install another Gallery app (like QuickPic), because the default android picture viewer does NOT support animated pictures. Sorry!
