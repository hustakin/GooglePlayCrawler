Adidas AG (German pronunciation: [ˈadiˌdas]) is a German multinational corporation that design and manufacture sports clothing and accessories based in Herzogenaurach, Bavaria, Germany. Adidas is the largest sportswear manufacturer in Germany and Europe and the second biggest sportswear manufacturer in the world.

Adidas was founded in 1948 by Adolf Dassler, following the split of Gebrüder Dassler Schuhfabrik between him and his older brother Rudolf.
