说明
Baby Monitor app or Baby sitting app, one of the best baby apps helps transform your phone into a tool to monitor your baby or a virtual baby sitter. It also works as a Baby Alarm which monitor baby and gives an alert when your baby is crying. This version of our Baby Monitor free, Baby Monitor Lite (primarily a Baby Sleep Monitor) is a completely Free Version.

Get a baby phone monitor and leave it along with your 'nanny phone' in your baby room to track your baby.

Baby monitor lets you record your own voice and play that back to the baby when he / she cries. Baby monitor logs all the monitoring events and displays it to the you.

Baby monitor rejects all incoming calls while monitoring. List of the rejected calls will be displayed at the end of monitoring.

Threshold calibration now takes care of the current noise level in the room. Baby monitor will let you set the threshold according to the current noise level.

The premium version of babymonitor which is yet to be launched will be coming with a nanny cam (av support) which gives you a baby watcher feature - allowing you to listen to your baby / see your baby / track your baby, even when you are away from home, making it one of the best baby monitor / baby listener app.

The current Baby Monitor free app helps you to choose a set of actions if your baby cries while you are away through monitoring your baby. Launch the app and place the phone near the baby to start monitoring. The app will monitor sound in baby's room and will immediately call or message you if there is a relevant noise, and allows you to communicate with your baby over speaker phone. Alternately, you can choose to play a song / lullaby to get your baby back to sleep.

The features available in this free babyphone app are listed below.

- Baby Listener Mode - Listen and talk/sing to your baby when your baby starts crying
- Adjustable sound levels. Baby monitor app allows you to choose what works best for your baby’s room.
- You can choose if the baby monitor app should alert parents using SMS or phone call
- You can choose if the baby monitor app should call the father or the mother.
- You can call to the phone in which baby monitor is installed from your configured number. Baby monitor will automatically connect the call and you can talk to the baby over speaker phone.
- You can configure the baby monitor app to play a song when the baby cries. If the baby is still crying after the song plays for a while, Baby Monitor app will call you. You can record your own voice too.
- Baby monitor app will monitor your baby even if the app is in background (till the "stop monitor" button is pressed)
- Baby monitor app will send an SMS incase baby monitor is not able to connect with parents over a call

The internet connection uses data volume. This might cause extra costs! Please ensure, that you buy appropriate data packages from your provider.

BATTERY CONSUMPTION:
The babyphone also continues running if the device is set to standby mode. This saves a lot of battery and elongate usage times. However, only the audio signal is transmitted during standby. Be aware that problems might occur if you have an app installed that cuts all internet connections on standby. It is highly encouraged to test standby mode first by setting the sender and receiver device to standby and creating some sound on the sender. The babyphone mobile app continues working also when it goes to background because someone is calling etc. Noise on the sender will still be transmitted to the receiver.

BROKEN CONNECTION AND DISCONNECTS:
The use of mobile internet connections might cause disconnects and broken connections from time to time. The App is designed to deal with temporary disconnects by automatically trying to reestablish the connection to the sender or receiver once a disconnect occurs. If it fails to reconnect, the user will be notified. However, to avoid frequent false alarms, connection problems are reported to the user only 15 to 60 seconds after they appeared as the connections can most often be automatically reestablished withing this time.
