**** For folks seeing a performance regression on 4.4 ****

Some longtime users of K-9 have reported incredibly slow message display times in 4.4. We haven't yet gotten our hands on a device that exhibits the problem, so we haven't been able to develop a proper fix. One thing that has worked for everyone who's tried it is so far to long-press on the account name in the Account list and select "Recreate Data" -- This will COMPLETELY ERASE the local copy of your mail and redownload a brand new copy of everything from your server. Be aware that any mail that's on your device but not on your server will be DELETED.

**********************************************************

K-9 supports IMAP, POP3 and Exchange 2003/2007 (with WebDAV).
