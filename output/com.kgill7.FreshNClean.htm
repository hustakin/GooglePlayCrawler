XHDPI/HDPI and Rooted Phones Only!

Please read the directions before leaving any feedback.

None of the comments will be answered on market, email instead.

Directions:

Download the theme,go to settings/themes and APPLY THE SYSTEM THEME AND REBOOT. Now select FreshNClean & apply. You may get an error: 'Sorry, this theme is missing assets for your device's screen size'.
