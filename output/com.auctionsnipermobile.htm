You can import eBay watches, place, edit and delete snipes using Auction Sniper for Android. You can also receive notifications via e-mail and SMS directly on your phone.

Try out Auction Sniper for free! New users are not charged for the first three auctions you win using Auction Sniper. Thereafter, we charge only 1% of the final auction price, with a minimum fee of $0.25 USD and a maximum fee of $9.95 USD, for only the auctions that you win.
