说明
Enhance your Kik experience with video messaging. Video for Kik allows you to send short video messages to your friends on Kik. You can also share your videos on Instagram, Facebook, Google+ and many other services right from the app!

Can't play videos?

If you have a device which cannot play videos make sure you have MX Player, MoboPlayer or another third party video player installed. A last resort is to open MX Player -> Setting -> Decoder -> S/W Decoder and make sure this option is Enabled.

Note: To make sure videos are compatible with all users we record them in a specific format. This is why you cannot select videos from your gallery.

This app is not endorsed by or affiliated with Kik Interactive, Inc.

无法播放视频？
 
如果你有一个设备不能发挥视频，请确保您有MX播放器，MoboPlayer或其他第三方视频播放器安装。最后一招，就是打开MX播放器 - >设置 - >解码器 - > S / W解码器，并确保这个选项被启用。
 
注意：要确保视频兼容所有用户，我们记录他们在一个特定的格式。这就是为什么你不能选择视频从您的画廊。
