说明
Smart Places lets you checkin to Facebook Places quickly, beautifully and even automatically.

* Checkin with just 2 clicks from your home screen with the 1x1 widget. Optionally tag friends.

* Draw zones on the map where you checkin automatically to any Facebook places of your choice (like Glympse but for space instead of time).

* Get notifications when your Facebook friends checkin nearby, and view recent Facebook checkins either on a Google Map or in list form. Never again miss out on the opportunity for real-world meet-ups that checkins bring!

* Other cool features include time stats for places which can be appended to checkin messages; and multiple location options, including WiFi, GPS and custom plot on the map before checkin.

See our Premium key for unlocking even more awesome features, including Priority Places, Photo Checkins, Auto-Checkouts, Privacy, 4x1 Widget, Custom Stat Messages, Time Targets, Satellite Maps, and get the ad-free experience of Smart Places.

Foursquare, Gowalla and Google+/Latitude checkin support is also planned, subject to demand.

Start using Facebook Places the smart way today, and checkin, checkin, checkin to Facebook!

Note: Requires Facebook permissions for access at any time ("may access your data when not using the app"), and for posting a checkin. We recommend that you also install the official Facebook for Android app, as this makes logging in easier and more reliable.
