说明
Precise Heart Rate PRO is one of its kind application, which helps you to view your heart beat on screen taken from Camera.

This app measures your heart beat rate automatically, when you place your index finger on the camera.

Note:
For cameras without flash light, take measurements in a bright environment. Provide ample lighting to pass through your fingers to the Camera. Blood flow should be visible on the Screen and the screen should be red in color.

This app can be used to measure heart beat in front camera available in tablets or in the primary camera of mobile phones/tablets.

Blood pressure is normal, when the heart beat is in the normal range provided in the Chart tab.

Blood pressure will be above normal, when the heart beat is very much beyond the normal range in the normal condition.

Features
✔ Automatically detects your heart beat by keeping your index finger on the camera.
✔ Save heart rate measured with date and time.
✔ View your heart beat on screen taken from Camera
✔ Configure alarm to notify you to measure your heart beat and check your health.
✔ Heart rate chart provided to identify any abnormalities in health.
