* * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *

This is an add-on pack for HD Widgets 3.0 & up.

IMPORTANT: THIS WILL NOT WORK WITHOUT HD WIDGETS!

HD Widgets 3.0 or later must be installed and running on your device.

You can download HD Widgets here:
https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=cloudtv.hdwidgets

* * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *

This pack contains high resolution weather icons for large tablet weather screens.

The detail is obvious with snowflakes or lightning. You won't see much of a difference with the sun & clouds. You may notice a little more detail with the moon.

HOW TO USE:

Just install it - HD Widgets will do the rest.

There's nothing else to open or see (so clicking on it won't do anything).


WHY DID WE MAKE THIS PACK?

We released this as a separate 16MB image pack is because if we put these together with the main app (16MB), the total would be over 32MB! Most devices will never use these extra-large icons, so why load them up?


Requirements:
HD Widgets 3.0 & later
1024x600 or more resolution tablet

重要提示：这将无法工作无高清WIDGETS！

高清小工具3.0或更高版本必须安装在您的设备上运行。

https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=cloudtv.hdwidgets

*************************

这个包包含大型平板天气屏幕高分辨率的天气图标。

细节是明显的雪花或闪电。你不会看到太多的差别与太阳和云彩。您可能会注意到一些更详细的月球。

如何使用：

只要安装它 - 高清小工具将做休息。

还有没有别的打开或看到（点击它不会做任何事情）。


为什么我们做了这个包？

我们放出了本作一个独立的16MB图像包是因为，如果我们把这些与主应用程序（16MB），总数将超过32MB！大多数设备永远不会使用这些额外的大图标，那么为什么加载它们？


要求：
高清小工具3.0及更高版本
分辨率为1024×600或以上平板
