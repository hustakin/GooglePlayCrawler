说明
FBReader (FBReaderJ) is an e-book reader. Supports epub, rtf, doc (MS Word), html, fb2(.zip), mobi (including non-protected kindle books), and plain text file formats.

FBReader is fast, highly customizable. Can use external TrueType/OpenType fonts and custom backgrounds. Screen brightness can be changed during reading (slide finger up/down along the left screen edge). Supports different day/night color schemes.

Includes a browser/downloader for network e-book catalogs/stores. Several popular English, French, Russian, Chinese and Polish libraries are included. Custom OPDS catalogs are supported too.

Alternatively you can download books manually and put them into /sdcard/Books catalog on your device.

FBReader is integrated with external dictionaries. Supports ColorDict, all Slovoed dictionaries (by Paragon Software), Fora Dictionary, FreeDictionary.org, LEO dictionary.

Localized for 29 languages. Includes hyphenation patterns for 24 languages. Open-source (GPL).

Similar applications: Kindle, Google Books, Aldiko, Cool Reader, Moon+ Reader, Alreader

Keywords: ebook, e-book, e-reader, reader, ePub, fb2, OPDS

FBReader（FBReaderJ）是一个电子书阅读器。支持EPUB，RTF，DOC，HTML，FB2（邮编），mobi手机（包括非保护Kindle电子书），纯文本文件格式（MS Word中）。

FBReader是速度快，高度可定制的。可以使用外部的TrueType / OpenType字体和自定义背景。屏幕的亮度可以在阅读过程中改变（向上/向下滑动手指沿屏幕左边缘）。支持不同的昼/夜配色方案。

包括一个浏览器/下载网络电子书目录/专卖店。一些流行的英语，法语，俄语，汉语和波兰语库都包括在内。支持自定义OPDS目录。

或者您也可以手动下载书籍到/ SD卡/您的设备上的图书目录，并把它们。

FBReader集成与外部字典。支持ColorDict，所有的SlovoEd词典（由Paragon软件），FreeDictionary.org，LEO论坛字典，词典。

本地化为29种语言。包括24种语言的断字模式。开源（GPL）。
