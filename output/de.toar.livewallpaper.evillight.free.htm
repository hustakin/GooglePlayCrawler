说明
Dream Create 3D is the most fascinating, thrilling and addictive interactive live wallpaper game on the market. Try and catch the lights whenever they arise. Or just watch - every wallpaper image of your choice will look outstanding with these beautiful light effects.

The wallpaper comes with pre-selected marvellous High Definition background pictures and plenty of configuration options for a highly individual and surprising self-made show you can create yourself.

This fully functional and 100% ad-free *free* version has limited configuration options. To get the full version with all options enabled, find DreamCreatorPro Live Wallpaper in the Android Market.

Play this game of light and awareness and test your reaction speed in various difficulty levels. Blow down the evil lights, if you are able to find them before it is too late. And be warned, they are hidden very well to start with!

When not playing, there is great animation to be watched, originating from and defined by the background image of your choice - if you feel like it, even taken directly from your phone's camera.

This interactive live wallpaper game operates smoothly and with very low power consumption on all tested devices. It can be installed/moved to SD-Card and comes with full support for landscape mode, home screen switching and all available screen resolutions.

Features:
- Realistic 3D effects in HD
- Free choice of background among the images on the phone.
- Beautiful build in background images (Sunset Lake, Snowy Forest, Dark Water, Shop,..)
- Multi-Lights-Mode.
- 'shrink after expansion'/blow out mode.
- Configurable growth time of light effects.
- User scores counted.
- Configurable maximum size of slow growth.
- Available for all screen resolutions.
- Screen rotation supported.
- Home screen switching supported.
- Low power/performance footprint.

To set Dream Create 3D as your live wallpaper, long-press on an empty space on your home screen, then select the 'Wallpaper' menu button, and 'Live Wallpapers'. From there, choose 'Dream Create 3D' and then click 'Set wallpaper'. To customize Options, select 'Configure' in the 'Wallpaper'-menu.

Enjoy!
