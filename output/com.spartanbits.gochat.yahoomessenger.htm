说明
Go!Chat for Yahoo! Messenger lets you talk with your friends via Yahoo! Messenger.

Enjoy lots of features while using the nice and clean interface from the acclaimed Go!Chat apps.

Features:

• Easy & Secure Yahoo! Messenger login
• Nice and clean user interface
• Yahoo! Smileys
• Display your contacts ordered by groups
• Create favorite contacts and receive notifications when they are online
• Rename your contacts
• Share images and location
• Send video, photos and voice notes easily over Yahoo! Messenger
• File transfer
• Swap conversations with gestures
• Homescreen Widget
• List and search all your Yahoo! Messenger contacts, even those who are offline (optional, set in app preferences)
• Send messages even when the other contact turns offline. Messages will be delivered as soon as he is back online!
• Themes: change the appearance of Go!Chat, you can choose from a nice set of themes (you'll have to download the Go!Chat Themes Plugin).
• No additional fees, 100% free, it only uses your internet data connection or WiFi.

Yahoo!, Yahoo! Messenger and the Yahoo! logo are registered trademarks from Yahoo!, Inc.

IMPORTANT: Widget disappears if app is installed on SD because a known Android issue. Move the app back to phone storage and reboot if you want to use it. This problem should be fixed by Google in next Android versions.
