说明
The Internet's first ever weather site, Weather Underground has built a reputation for providing the most reliable and in-depth weather data for locations across the world. With our unique network of 22,000+ neighborhood weather stations our app provides the most localized weather information available.

What makes this weather app different from other apps?

Local, Local, Local
- Our 22,000+ personal weather stations report the most localized weather conditions with updates as often as every 2.5 seconds
- Interactive WunderMap
The most interactive weather map on mobile allows you to select from different weather overlays including current conditions from our personal weather station network, animated radar and satellite and webcams.
Features
- Current conditions from your local weather station include temperature, ‘feels like’ temperatures, wind speed and direction, humidity, dew point, visibility, pressure, gusts and severe weather
- 7-day Forecasts including text summary
- Hourly Forecasts
- Interactive Maps showing all weather stations in your neighborhood with animated radar and satellite
- List view of saved Favorite Locations and Recent Searches
- International forecasts use Weather Underground’s proprietary forecast system which ingests data from our personal weather station network
- Statusbar notification for a favorite location -- Shows current conditions and temperature
- Search for individualized forecasts for each of our Personal Weather Stations

Tips
- Add/Remove favorites using the settings menu
- Tap a temperature icon on the WunderMap to view detailed current conditions for that location
- Long press a location in the 'recent' view to add location to favorites
- Long press a location in the 'favorites' view to set as the statusbar notification location
- Toggle statusbar notifications on/off from the settings menu
- Search for a Personal Weather Station by typing 'PWS:pwsname' in the search bar. Example -- 'PWS:KCASANFR70'

特点
- 您当地的天气站从目前的情况，包括温度，“感觉一样的温度，风速和方向，湿度，露点温度，能见度，气压，风和恶劣天气

Rain coming? Check Raindar! Animated Doppler radar on Google Maps for the US. Now also on Google TV and tablet!

Going somewhere and you're not sure if you need to bring your umbrella? Want to check if there's going to be fresh powder snow on the slopes in Tahoe tomorrow? Check Raindar! Raindar displays animated Doppler radar images on your Google Map. When you start Raindar it automatically focuses on your location so you can quickly see if there is precipitation coming your way. When pressing the search button on your phone you can now also search for locations within the US!

Raindar works out of the box but has many configuration options (see the recent changes page).

Color:
- green/yellow/red is rain
- blue/white is snow

Raindar can now also display storm tracks! It displays different icons for each of the 4 types of storms:

- Yellow circle: thunderstorm
- Yellow square: Hail Storm
- Yellow diamond: Rotating Thunderstorm (Mesocyclone)
- Magenta triangle: Tornado Vortex Signature (pretty heavy!)

When clicking on one of those icons a dialog will appear with detailed information.
The storm tracks also display the direction and speed of the storm in the form of an arc. The arc is split in 3 parts: 20, 40 and 60 minutes until the storm reaches that area.

On Google TV:
- Use the D-pad for navigate up/down/left/right
- Use the media forward/back or next/previous buttons to zoom in and out

Radar images and storm track data courtesy of Weather Underground. Raindar displays images from the WunderMap.
Geocoding courtesy of GeoNames.org.
