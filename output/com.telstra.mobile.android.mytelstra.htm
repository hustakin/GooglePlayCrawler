If you are a Telstra consumer customer, you can manage your Telstra services in one place, with the Telstra 24x7® App.

Simply download the app, enter your My Account details and you’re ready to go! To set up My Account, tap the Register button in the login screen after installing the app or alternatively visit telstra.com/myaccount from a PC.

Please note, Business account information is not currently available through My Account and therefore cannot be viewed in this app.

Once you’ve set up your My Account profile, the app will allow you to:
• View your current and past bill summaries.
• Tap through to pay your bills.
• Keep tabs on your estimated usage on post-paid mobile plans or select BigPond® broadband plans and Telstra home phone services.
• View estimated usage for your Pre-Paid and post-paid mobile services directly from the home screen with the Widget.
• Purchase a Data Pack.
• View your international roaming status.
• Tap through to apply to activate International roaming and purchase data packs for use while overseas.
• Tap through to manage Messagebank and premium SMS settings for the service you are using.
• Take advantage of the THANKS™ discounted movie ticket offer.
• View details for suspended post-paid services.

If you’ve got a Pre-Paid service, the app will allow you to:
• Check your balance.
• Recharge your service.
• Purchase a Plus Pack.
• Tap through to change the offer of the service you are using.
• View detailed usage and recharge history.

Whatever services you have with Telstra, you can also use the app to:
• Locate your nearest Telstra store.
• Get 24x7 support via our social media teams and live chat.
• Customise your app with service nicknames and colour theme.

Standard data charges apply to download this App. Telstra mobile customers will not incur data usage charges for viewing or using this App unless roaming overseas, using the Store Locator with maps turned on, or accessing help and support via 24x7 social support, or where you link through to the Telstra THANKS™ offers. If you are not a Telstra mobile customer, data charges may apply. Please check with your mobile service provider.
