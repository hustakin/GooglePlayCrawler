Record with your mobile a few seconds of music you listen on radio, on television or in any other place, and retrieve this music directly into your Deezer music application.

With only seven seconds of recorded music, 'Stream that song' find in a database of several million of entries the name of the song you heard. On demand, 'Stream that song' is able to launch your Deezer mobile application to play this song.

'Stream that song' needs a network connection (WiFi or 2G/3G) to analyze your record, but if we do not have a network connection when recording'Stream that song' store it for further analysis.
