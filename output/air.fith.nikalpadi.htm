Slumdog Plumber & Pipes Puzzle


When a man’s gotta go, a man’s gotta go!


Slumdog Plumber & Pipes Puzzle (Nikal Padi) is an exciting, funny and occasionally stinky pipe puzzle game where you must figure out the right arrangement for the plumbing so that our man waiting at the tap can get his pail of water and attend to his “urgent business”!

Fail to get the water before time runs out, and our poor constipated friend will have an “accident”! Succeed and you will see him relieved. So don’t keep him waiting! Download and install the game, and play nerve-racking missions of intense fun, farts and laughter with Slumdog Plumber & Pipes Puzzle.


Game instructions

Design a continuous pipeline between the valve and water outlet by clicking on the pipe to rotate.

Once the proper connections have been made, release water and proceed to the next level.

Make sure you connect the pipes correctly before time runs out, otherwise there will be an "accident."


*In case you are wondering, Nikal Padi is a Hindi slang with different contexts. In this case, it means that if you fail to achieve your goal, $hit happens. Pun intended.
