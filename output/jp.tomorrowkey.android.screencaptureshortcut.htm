What can I do with the “Folder listener” trigger?
If your device has built-in screenshot capability you can set the app to listen for changes to a folder. You could also use another screenshot app, like Screenshot UX, Screenshot Free, Screenshot and Draw, Screenshot ER or Screenshot It, and use Screenshot Ultimate to handle the editing / saving.

No “Capture Method” available for my device!
This is normal if your device isn't rooted. It's the way how the Android security model works; it just doesn't allow taking a screenshot on some devices. As soon as an image is created in that folder Screenshot Ultimate Pro will also trigger (for editing, saving to your own folder, etc). You could also use another screenshot app, like Screenshot UX, Screenshot Free, Screenshot and Draw, Screenshot ER or Screenshot It, and use Screenshot Ultimate Pro to handle the editing and saving.

No “Capture Method” is available for my device!
This is normal if your device isn't rooted. It's the way how the Android security model works; it just doesn't allow taking a screenshot on some devices. However, you can enable screenshot functionality on your device by enabling it through a computer. To see how you can do this go to “Screenshot Ultimate Pro” > “Settings” > “Capture methods” > “No capture method help”. You can email the manual to yourself so you can complete it on your computer (Windows, Linux or Mac OSX).
