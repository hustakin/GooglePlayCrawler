说明
Easily record and archive sounds, noises, or ideas with your phone. This app keeps an easy to use archive list of your sounds, so you can store them in the archive, and use them as custom ringtones, notifications, or alarm sounds!

Formerly known as Easy Ringtone Maker, the app lets you record and archive friends talking, babies laughing, burps, sirens, screams, or any weird, funny noise you can find, and put it on your phone as a ringtone! The app allows unlimited numbers of recordings and archiving, and is all free.

The interface is designed to be very simple and easy to use. Just select a sound file, record, and it is stored in your personal archive. With the press of a button, you can:

- set a recording as the main ringtone
- set it as your notification or alarm
- use different custom ringtones for people on your contact list
- It has an easy to use editing feature that lets you cut out only the part of a recording that you want
- It keeps a saved list of your favorite recordings, so you can easily switch between ringtones

No more confusing, difficult programs to try to figure out!

You can also use it as a soundboard, and keep those hilarious archived sounds ready when you need them. Easy Ringtone Maker is one of the easiest to use ringtone makers available! All free (banner ad supported).
