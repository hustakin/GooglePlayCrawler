说明
SMS Notifier (SMS Popup) displays your SMS or message in a smart and simple way. It provides a better status bar notification and SMS popup with quick reply, delete and mark as read options. A very small app with low memory consumption. You can customise SMS notifier very easily.

Features:
- Elegant popup window on incoming SMS/message. Android ICS like theme for all users.
- Mark as read, delete, reply directly from the sms popup.
- Reply directly from SMS Popup using quick reply option.
- Use 4 themes for SMS Popup - Black, White, Blue and Pink to enhance your experience with SMS.
- Customizable settings for individual contacts.
- Popup shows contact name, contact number, time, sms content, contact picture.
- Use your own or provided preset SMS for even quicker reply.
- Contact option on sms popup like call, messaging etc.
- Customize sms popup notification by setting vibration or particular ringtone.
- A better status bar notification.
- Group notification on android status bar.
- Unread SMS count with contact name/number on android status bar.

Note:- Please disable android default messaging sms notification. Go to android messaging app, then press Menu key, choose Settings and uncheck Notifications option.
