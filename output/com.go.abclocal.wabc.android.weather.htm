With AccuTrack WABC NY AccuWeather, when threatening weather is nearby your phone can be set to alert you!

Weather alerts are read aloud for you so you don’t have to stop to read the screen. It’s the power of a NOAA Weather Radio combined with the accuracy of AccuWeather and Channel 7 all in one convenient app! There’s no other app like it.

With the FOLLOW ME feature you’ll stay safe when you’re on the go. AccuTrack WABC NY AccuWeather can alert you to severe weather wherever you are -- whether you’re on your daily commute or travelling anywhere across the U.S.

The app is fully interactive; Zoom down to get a street level view of live conditions. And of course, get current conditions, hourly and long-term forecasts right at your fingertips.

FEATURES:

* Follow Me – Activate this feature so that you get weather alerts wherever you are, anywhere in the U.S. No matter where you travel, the app will help keep you safe.

* Set a static location – In addition to the Follow Me feature, you can set one location to receive alerts on. So if you’re on the road, you could still get alerts for what’s threatening your home. If you’d like to keep tabs on a vacation home or where a loved one lives, additional locations can be purchased for a nominal fee within the app.

* Audio alerts – Listen to weather alerts just like a NOAA Weather Radio, so you can be informed and keep moving.

* Current Conditions, Hourly Forecasts and 7 Day Forecasts

* Video forecasts and live weather video (when available)
