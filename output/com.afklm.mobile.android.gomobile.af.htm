Organize your trip through your mobile phone: purchase your ticket, check in and keep informed in real time… no matter where you are!

Plan your journey
Prepare your trip with access to schedules and connection times to and from any destination. You can also conveniently access all contact information for AIR FRANCE, KLM and Flying Blue around the world.

Purchase your ticket
Book your next flight from your mobile phone in just a few clicks! Access all AIR FRANCE KLM destinations through our booking search engine, and rest assured that our online credit and debit card payment system is completely secure. You can book a flight for several people at the same time.
Schedules in real time
Stay informed on flight delays, strikes and cancellations by consulting flight information updated in real time. If you plan on picking up a friend or family member at the airport, please remember to check the arrival time of his or her flight!

Check-in
Check in to your flight and any possible connecting flights. You can also check in all travelers in your group and enjoy a discount on the purchase of any additional baggage allowance.
To choose your seat, select it on the cabin map. If you would like more leg room, simply choose Seat Plus.
For your flight to Europe or North Africa and Israel, you can choose to receive your boarding pass in the form of a flash code available directly on your mobile phone. Simply present this flash code at each security checkpoint at the airport. You can also choose to print your boarding pass at home or at the airport.

Change in plans? Please remember to cancel your booking to modify your itinerary.


Manage your booking
Manage your booking and access your trip’s complete details directly from your mobile phone! Simply enter your ticket or booking number, or your Flying Blue card number. Do you have a modifiable ticket? Modify your booking in only a few clicks, with or without fees according to the conditions of your ticket.

Your Flying Blue Miles
Review your Miles balance as well as the number of additional Miles you need to rise to the next Flying Blue level.

Your personalized interface
Once you have booked your ticket, take advantage of a personalized interface!
We will only offer the services that are most likely to interest you, such as Seat Plus, additional baggage allowances, or flight modifications.
Are you a Flying Blue member? Your personalized interface will appear once your booking has been issued.
Not yet a Flying Blue member? To access your personalized interface, simply enter your ticket or booking number in the app’s “My bookings” section. You will also have automatic access once you have checked in online.

Frequently Asked Questions
To find answers to your questions, don’t hesitate to visit our FAQ section. Ask your question directly, search by theme or view the list of top 10 questions!
