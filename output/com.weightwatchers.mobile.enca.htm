Q: Where does nutrition and exercise science for the app come from?
A: We have a dedicated team of dieticians, exercise scientists, personal trainers, and behavior experts that program their knowledge into the Noom Weight Loss Coach.
Q: What if I already use CardioTrainer or Calorific?
A: If you already use CardioTrainer, Noom will NOT erase your data, and you can transfer it using your permanent account. Noom will not share data from CardioTrainer or Calorific, so if you want to record new tracks or food, be sure to do so from Noom.
