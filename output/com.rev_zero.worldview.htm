Sets your wallpaper to a realistic image of the Earth with real-time display of clouds, nighttime city lights, local time, and more. View the world as a map or globe, follow the sunrise or sunset, or scroll to any location by double-tapping the home screen.

By downloading this app, you agree to the EULA at http://rev-zero.com/eula/worldview

INSTRUCTIONS: This is a live wallpaper, so it doesn't show up in the regular application drawer. If you are using Globe View, or Map View without a tracking mode configured, you can double-tap the home screen to scroll to and stop at the desired central location.
