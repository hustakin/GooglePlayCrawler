说明
G-Stomper Beat Studio is a Music Production Tool, highly optimized for doing Electronic Live Performances in Studio Quality. It's a feature packed, Step Sequencer based Drum Machine/Groovebox, a Track Grid Sequencer for Beats, a Note Grid Sequencer for Melodies, a Piano Roll, 24 Drum Pads, an Effect Rack, a Master Section, a Line Mixer and a Live Pattern/Song Arranger. Wherever you are, take your mobile device and start straight away to create your music.

** IMPORTANT for GALAXY S3 : DISABLE AUTO-HAPTIC **
** settings > sound > auto-haptic > disable **

If you have any issues, please check the FAQs at http://www.planet-h.com/gstomper/gstfaq.html or email to gstomper@planet-h.com
For any further support join the Support Forum at: http://www.planet-h.com/gstomperbb/

• Pattern based Drum Machine/Step Sequencer with up to 24 Tracks (the free demo is limited to 12)
• Create Patterns with a length of 1..128 steps / 1..8 bars (with variable bar length of 1..16 steps), use various Beat Signatures up to 64th (quadruple) or 32th triple with a tempo from 60.0..300.0 bpm, apply complex Swing quantization (selective steps), program or tap record your Beats, shift/move them around or edit them step by step, apply cool auto-generated Fill-Ins
• 8-Octave Piano Roll (switchable) in Note Edit Mode
• Note Grid: Matrix like Melodic Sequencer
• Track Grid: Multi Track Step Sequencer
• Drum Pads: 24 Multi-Touch Pads for live playing
• Line Mixer: Up to 24 Channels
• Effect Rack: 3 chainable Effect Processors (28 Effect Types)
• Master Section: 1 Sum Effect Processor (24 Effect Types)
• Effect Types: 3 Compressor/Limiter types, 7 Resonant Filters, Chorus/Flanger, 2 Phaser types, Vibrato, Auto Panner, X-Delay, Short Delay, Reverb, Pitch Shifter, Stereo Enhancer, Exciter, Bottom Booster, Bit Crusher, Waveshaper, Distortion, Bender Distortion, 2 Equalizers
• Effect Specials: Side Chain Support, Tempo sync, Tempo synchable LFO's, Envelope Followers
• Track Filters: Up to 24 12dB Filter Sections (1 per Track)
• Live Pattern/Song Arranger (Pattern Set) with up to 64 concurrent Patterns
• High Quality Audio Engine (32bit float DSP algorithms)
• Real-Time Sample Modulation: Panorama, Volume, Start Point, Length, Pitch, Sample Reverse, Envelope, and many more..
• Full Motion Sequencing/Automation Support
• Factory Drum Kits (full version): 909, S09, 808, 707, 606/CR8000, 505, RZ-1, Electro, Linn, Rock
• Factory Drum Kits (demo version): 909, Electro
+ a small collection of vocals, synthesizer, and bass sounds
• User Sample Support (load your own samples): Uncompressed wav, aif/aiff, snd, Bit Rates: 8..64bit, any Sample Rate (tested from 6kHz to 192kHz)
• Support for additional Sample-Packs (full version): Many Packs available
• Sample Bouncing, 8..32bit: (full version): Bounce single samples incl. their Parameters to new WAV files
• Track Bouncing, 8..32bit: (full version): Bounce complete Tracks incl. all EFX and Modulations to audio Loops
• WAV File Export, 8..32bit (full version): Export your Loops/Songs as Sum or Track by Track and use them in Ableton, FL or any Digital Audio Workstation of your choice.
