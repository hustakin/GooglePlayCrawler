* recipients can be typed in directly or selected from contacts
* multiple recipients can be chosen
* flexible scheduling system - you can send sms once, every 5 minutes, 15 minutes, every hour ...

* status bar notifications are triggered for sent messages and delivery reports
* history of sent and delivered messages is available

****** USAGE ******

You can edit every entry just by clicking on it. To add a new entry use a button on the bottom of a main screen. To see a status of last 1000 sms dispatches and deliveries press Menu button on the main screen and choose History. Every entry in the list specifies a recipient, original date and time on which sms should be sent, real date and time on which sending or delivery occurs and status of the whole operation ("Sent without problems" or "Successfully delivered" means that everything went well).
In the menu on the main screen you can also choose "Preferences" option which lets you enable/disable notifications and conversation threads insertion.
