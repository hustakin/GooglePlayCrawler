说明
Find available tables, make reservations instantly and discover restaurants with OpenTable for Android. With over 20,000 bookable restaurants in the US, Canada & Mexico, OpenTable helps you spend less time waiting and more time enjoying your meal.


Features:

Find restaurants near you – or wherever you’re headed
- Search by location, date, time and party size.
- See what’s available in real-time – on a list or a map.

Refine your search results
- Sort restaurants by distance, name or star ratings.
- Filter by price, 1,000-point tables, favorite neighborhoods and cuisines.

Check out menus, photos & trusted reviews
- Read helpful reviews – all reviews are from restaurant guests who have booked & dined there.
- View restaurant menus and photos of top dishes.

Instantly book your table
- Pick a restaurant, tap the time you want and the table is yours.
- Include special requests, like “It’s our anniversary!” or “I’m allergic to nuts”.

Invite friends to dinner & stay on top of plans
- Send OpenTable invitations to anyone from your device.
- Add reservations to your calendar. Change of plans? Update reservations in the app.

Dine out, get rewarded
- Earn points with every reservation – like airline miles for dining out.
- Redeem points for dining cheques, which are accepted at all OpenTable restaurants.
