To keep you stay updated, we've added push notification to the apps so it'll notify you for any headline update. You can deactivate this function on Settings (from android Menu).

You can search recent news by using keyword directly from the Apps.

Now you can zoom in or zoom out text in news detail, so you'll be more comfortable when reading the news

- Shake to Contact Support
Give us a shout, complain, error, bug report, or any suggestion about the apps by just shaking the phone.

Now you can watch MetroTV Live TV streaming directly from your device

- VoD Streaming
If you've missed MetroTV show from your TV, then you can watch it anytime you want from this apps
