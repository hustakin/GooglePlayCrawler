Photo 360° is the first application on Google Play which lets users take fantastic 360° panoramic photos without taking multiple pictures or any other difficult manipulation. Record the environment around you and capture both the image and sound all in one revolution.

Thanks to its simple use, Photo 360° immortalizes your best moments anywhere at any time by taking “magical” 360 degree photos.

Three kinds of 360° pictures can be taken turning clockwise:

1- 360 degree Panorama: You will obtain in a few seconds a full 360 degree panorama (without any discrepancy problems) by just holding your device.
2- 360 degree object/person photo: Get a 360°view of an object (or person) simply by turning around.
3- “Behind you” photo (available for front-facing camera devices): Create an amazing picture that will show the world behind you thanks to the front camera of the device (through the app).
Features:
• Share your photos on Facebook and Twitter
• Possibility to view your photos played with Flash (integrating the sound sequence) via the Photo 360° Web Viewer.
• Possibility to take landscape photos.
• GPS location record
• Possibility to send the photo’s URL (once uploaded) to a friend by email.

IMPORTANT: Please note that without good lighting photos can often be blurred or be difficult to capture. For high quality images we recommend outdoor areas.
