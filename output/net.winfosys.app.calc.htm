你可以计算自己的体重指数和腰围身高比值，根据年龄和性别找出你的理想体重。

• 适用于7岁及以上人群
• 支持公制和英制系统
• 选择性保存上一次输入值

简介：
• 体重指数：是用体重除以身高平方得出的数值。该数值提供了身高与体重之间关系的导向，但并没有把个体因素考虑在内，如肌肉发达程度。

• 腰围身高比：是用腰围除以身高得出的数值。该数值测量体内脂肪的分配。最近的研究指出，该数值同时是检测心脏疾病风险一个更好的指标。

• 体脂率：是用脂肪重量除以总体重得出的数值。该数值通过被称为“海军身材测量方法”的方法估算得出，此方法使用了身高、腰围、颈围和臀围的数值。

Now optimized for your tablet, the Trulia Real Estate App let’s you lean back and enjoy your home search. With full-screen interactive maps and high-res photo slideshows, it’s the most pleasing way to get the real estate info you crave.

“One of the best Android apps for finding a house to buy, place to rent, or to simply browse local real estate information." – Best Android Apps Review

Note: Trulia Real Estate & Rentals only provides listings in the US at this time.

Key Features:
✔ Instant Maps: Using built-in GPS, see nearby homes for sale, apartments for rent or recently sold homes
✔ Open Houses: Find open homes for sale near you, and easily add them to your calendar
✔ Custom Searches: Add search filters like price, lot size, number of beds, neighborhood, and more.
✔ Keyword Searches: Search for listings using keywords or MLS numbers
✔ Photo slideshows: View slideshows of homes, condos, townhomes, apartments, and more
✔ Instant Updates: Get notified immediately when new home listings come up in your favorite areas or there are status updates to your saved homes
✔ Local Scoop: See school attendance zones and nearby amenities like restaurants, grocery stores, gas stations and more
✔ Crime Maps: See a crime map of any neighborhood in the US
✔ Map Modes: View a map area in normal, satellite or terrain mode
✔ Mortgage Calculator: Get personalized rates and calculate your mortgage
✔ Cross-device Access: Log in to access your favorite searches and properties across all your devices
✔ Connect w/Agents: Quickly contact relevant real estate agents about listings
✔ Directions: Get directions to open houses, homes for sale, or apartments for rent
✔ Trulia Widget: See new homes for sale, right from your home screen
✔ Samsung Air View: Hover your finger - or S-pen - on a property listing to see property details (only available on Samsung GALAXY Note I, Note II, Note 8.0 and 10.1, and Samsung GALAXY S4)
