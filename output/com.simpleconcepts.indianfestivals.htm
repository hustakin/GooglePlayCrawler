说明
A simple to use application that provides list of major Indian festivals grouped by Month. The application includes festivals and dates for 2012, 2013 and 2014 (i.e. last Year, this year and next year). You can use this application to plan for your festivals and holidays.

Features -
- Calendar monthly view with all Indian festival dates specially marked
- Long Tap on the event in Month or year view to add to Android Calendar
- Monthly view also displays the festivals in list view on the bottom half
- Swipe your finger across the calendar to change month or use navigation button to change month
- Festivals listed for each year in the List tab. You can navigate between years to see festivals for the respective year
