The Yandex.News widget helps you keep up-to-date with the day's main events in Russia, Ukraine, Kazakhstan and Belarus.
When you click on a news headline, the full article will open in a web version of Yandex.News that has been specially adapted for Android.
The widget can be used in both compact (1x4) and full-screen (4x4) mode. In compact mode, it automatically scrolls through the news headlines, while full-screen mode lets you browse the news by a topic of your choice.
The news feed is updated 7 days a week, 24 hours a day, and all the stories are provided by leading news agencies.

- Order a cab without calling the dispatcher – your order is sent directly to the drivers in your area so they reach you as quickly as possible.
- The app shows all the necessary information about the driver and their car.
- Refine your search, for example, smoker or non-smoker, children’s seat, receipt etc.
- Track the location of your car on the map,
- Get price estimate in the cities supporting this feature,
- Payment by credit card and Yandex.Money in Moscow,
- Rate your journey, which will affect the overall score of the driver.
The app is currently supported in Moscow, Saint Petersburg, Kazan, Voronezh, Krasnoyarsk and Vologda.
