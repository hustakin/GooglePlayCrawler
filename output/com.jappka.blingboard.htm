说明
BlingBoard is an all in one social widget that combines your Missed calls, SMS, Gmail, Twitter and Facebook widgets in one android widget on your home screen.

With BlingBoard you can stay updated with your friends, colleagues and family, and interact with them quickly and effortlessly.

It’s free, fun and easy to use!

THE PRESS
"BlingBoard is a really nice, easy-to-use app that centralise all your main communication activity"(TheNextWeb)

"The ultimate social widget for Android bringing everything from Gmail, Twitter, missed calls and more into one easy to use and beautiful widget." (AndroidCommunity)

"Blingboard is a stunning little widget that looks great and is both convenient to glance at email, calls, text and social networks plus it is fun to use" (AndroidTapp)

2 WIDGET SIZES:
4x2 Widget with messages content
4x1 Notification Widget

WHAT SOCIAL NOTIFICATIONS
- Missed Calls
- SMS Messages
- Gmail unread count
- Facebook news feeds (similar to Facebook widget)
- Twitter feed items (similar to Twitter widget)

REPLY INSTANTLY
- Dial to your Missed Calls
- Reply to SMS messages
- Launch Gmail
- Facebook: Check links directly from the widget (YouTube videos will launch the YouTube App, etc..)
- Twitter: Check links directly from tweets
- Simply launch the related app when no notifications.

EASY ON THE EYES
Designed from the grounds-up with simplicity in mind without compromising functionality.

FREE
Did we mention it’s free?

HOW TO INSTALL
After downloading BlingBoard you will need to add the widget to your homescreen.
At your home screen: Menu > Add > Widgets > BlingBoard.
For more help please visit our website: http://jappka.com/Android/BlingBoard/faq.html

CLEAR OPTION
Use the sweep button to clear notifications from the selected tab. The clear button will remove items from BlingBoard widget only.

NOTES
- Up to 20 message/feed items for each tab
- Up to 140 characters for each message/feed item
- SMS: only new sms messages will be displayed, SMS messages prior to BlingBoard installation will NOT be displayed

User's Guide to Winstagram:

Winstagram enables you to view your Instagram Photos via:
1.Instagram
2.Statigram
3.Webstagram

websites. Just click on the icon of website you want to login.

How to Add Photo's to HomeScreen Widgets:

1. Go to Widgets from your homescreen, choose Winsta1, Winsta2, Winsta3, or Winsta4, and drop it on your homescreen. Then you will see the widgets on your homescreen showing just Winstagram logo for now. If you click on it now, nothing will change yet unless you add photo to widgets by following the steps below. You can add all the 4 widgets together at the same time to your home screen.

2. Open Winstagram app

3. Login to Instagram, Statigram, or Webstagram

4. Go to the photo's page you want to add to homescreen widget.
If you want to retrieve the photo and see it full size, click Menu->View Image
You can crop the picture using the four buttons right to menu.

YOU SHOULD FIRST PUSH ONE OF THE CROP BUTTONS (TO THE RIGHT OF THE "Menu" BUTTON) BEFORE YOU PUSH "Set Page/Img to Widget" BUTTONS

5. While on the photo page, click Menu->Set Page/Img to Widget1
(or Set Page/Img to Widget2, Set Page/Img to Widget3, Set Page/Img to Widget4 for adding images to relative widgets)

6. Click on the widget on your homescreen and the photo you added to your widget will appear on your homescreen now.

What do Menu buttons do:

Home Page : Navigates to opening page
Set Page/Img to Widget1 : Adds photo to Winsta1
Set Page/Img to Widget2 : Adds photo to Winsta2
Set Page/Img to Widget3 : Adds photo to Winsta3
Set Page/Img to Widget4 : Adds photo to Winsta4
View Image : Retrieves a photo and shows it full size. You will see just the photo when you click that button. This button appears only if you are at the photo's page on Instagram or Webstagram.
Four buttons right to menu button : They are used for taking snapshot of the webpage you are currently viewing and cropping the snapshot or photo. After finishing cropping, you should push on one of the Set Page/Img to Widget buttons.
