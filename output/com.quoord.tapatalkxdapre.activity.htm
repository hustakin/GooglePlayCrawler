For devices with cutsom ROMs
WiFi direct doesn't play well with custom ROMs. Please follow these steps to switch to hotspot (tethering) mode if WiFi direct is causing you issues:
1- Open SuperBeam scanner.
2- Tap options > settings.
3- Tap Advanced settings > Preferred direct method.
4- Choose Hotspot.
5- Close SuperBeam and try sharing again.

A note for advanced users
SuperBeam creates an access point using Android's WiFi direct API's. This essentially works like hotspot mode in old devices, but it is guaranteed to work on newer devices like Nexus 7.
