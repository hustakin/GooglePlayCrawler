说明
Check weather forecasts and current conditions, time and date for any location. And you can check 5 days detailed forecasts.
Plus, you can check the current weather and the rain fall probability in the status bar.
(new!)And you can check the rain radar, satellite view and map for the location.

(Please feel free to send any request and question by email. We are always welcome to hear them and improve our aplication.)
* If this application is not found in the list of the widget, please check whether this is moved to SD card or not, then please move to the internal storage.
* The usage of the reconfiguration is explained on the bottom.

* Ver5.18 Ver5.20 Ver5.21 has a critical bug, please update the application to the latest version.

Features:
* GPS based forecast (Including wind, humidity, rain probability)
* You can check the rain probability and weather forecasts all over the world (197+ countries).
* Show the forecast in the status bar.
* 3 icon styles and 4 backgrounds
* Weekly weather forecasts
* Compact display mode for incompatible handsets
* Show a day, day of the week and year in the widget
* Settings for refresh rate and time adjustment
* Translations for 20+ languages

Notice:
*The location of GPS and forecast information are updated every one hour. If you want to update now, please tap "Update now" button.
*If there are some errors, please reinstall the app then reboot the handset.
*If timezone is different, please send it by e-mail.
*Rain radar is cached for 1 hour.

Usage:
* How to reconfigure the widget settings
1. Tap a widget and open weekly forecasts
