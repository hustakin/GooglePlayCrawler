A spectacular live wallpaper of storm clouds and lightning, Thunderstorm uses OpenGL to render a constantly moving scene of clouds. Works as either a live wallpaper or a Daydream!

This paid version has a full settings screen, so you can customize ambient light color, bolt color, wind speed, bolt frequency, number of clouds, and more! Enable colors to change by time of day, or go retro with minimalist mode!

Tap your screen to summon lightning!

PERMISSIONS:
- Coarse Location allows calculation of reasonable sunrise/sunset times for your current location
