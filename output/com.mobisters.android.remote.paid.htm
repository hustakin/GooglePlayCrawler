“I’m almost there, come out” – if this sentence sounds familiar, then our application is right for you!

Now, instead of looking up the phone number, dialing it, and saying that standard phrase (all while you’re driving), all you need to do is drive up to the place, and your phone will do all the work for you!

★ How this works ★
You set up quick-launch shortcuts; each of them contains one geographic location (specified on the map or through an address search) and one contact (chosen from the Contact List on your phone) who will be called at the moment when you drive up to the selected location.

In order not to exhaust your phone’s battery by constantly using the GPS, we have developed the One Click To Go system – before leaving, you select the desired shortcut and indicate how long it will be active in minutes (from 1 to 4 hours). As soon as you reach the place or the timer stops, the system automatically turns the GPS off.
