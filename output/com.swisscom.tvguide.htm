Swisscom TV offers everything you need for best television viewing while on the move – NOW also available on tablets: Choose from over 80 channels and enjoy exclusive live sporting events. Use the Replay function to watch missed programmes (up to 30 hours later) and also enjoy your photos while on the go (with Swisscom TV plus). The remote control function allows you to operate your Swisscom TV box at home.

Key functions:
- Free Live TV on over 80 Channels with dual tone
- Replay function (30 hours)
- Programming and viewing of recordings
- Live sporting events
- Information on TV programmes, live sporting events and top films on demand
- Remote control for Swisscom TV

LIVE TV
Select your favourite programmes from over 80 channels; with dual tone where this is supported. (1)

You can access the full TV guide for over 300 channels anytime as well as information on live sporting events and top films on demand. Your personal channel line-up and bookmarked films on the Swisscom TV box are taken over automatically.

With a Swisscom TV plus subscription you can record any programme while you’re on the go and watch it at home or wherever you like. (3)

Connect the app to the Swisscom TV box and use it as a remote control (4). This is particularly practical when replaying recorded programmes or watching Video-on-Demand.
