No manual entries. No typos. No more confusion in your contacts.

- You can only save 50 cards in card holder after recognition. (paid version has no limitation)

- While importing WorldCard Exchange file (wcxf file), it will only save the first 20 cards. (paid version has no limitation)
- Cannot export contacts to vCard file.

**While you restore in paid version, the card images of lite version will be replaced.
