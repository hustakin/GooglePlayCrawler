说明
A picture frame widget which shows random pictures from your phone (camera photos by default). You can also display a single photo in a custom size.

* 9 sizes on 4x4 screens (14 on 5x5 screens)
* Select frequency of changes
* Skip a picture
* Never show a picture again
* Open the photo in gallery
* Optional frame
* Select a single image
* Choose to show jpg, png, or both
* Sizes can be removed from widget list

To use: Menu -> Add -> Widgets on home screen
