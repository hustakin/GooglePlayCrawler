CM9/AOKP THEME [HDPI & XHDPI DEVICES]

Welcome!!

This theme is based on the new SonyEricsson Xperia UI for ICE Cream Sandwich.

REQUIREMENTS:

For apply this theme you need a custom ROM CM9/AOKP.

INSTRUCTTIONS:

Step 1. Download theme from Google Play, go to settings/themes and APPLY SYSTEM THEME AND REBOOT.

Step 2. Now come back to settings/themes , select & apply my Theme. You may get an error: 'Sorry, this theme is missing assets for your device's screen size'. Ignore that and apply it anyway

Step 3. Restart your device.

Step 4. Enjoy!!


If you have any problem, please contact me for mail or visit http://www.htcmania.com/showthread.php?p=4759942#post4759942 and I will try my best to make everything work.

Thank you!!
