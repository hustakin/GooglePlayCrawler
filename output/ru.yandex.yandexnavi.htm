说明
Find your way around town or the entire country using only the fastest routes and clearest roads. This app offers turn-by-turn directions for Russia and Ukraine and can plan journeys taking current traffic conditions, closures etc. into account.

With the Yandex.Navigator app you can:
— Automatically update the road map monthly;
— Access the always up-to-date online map on your device;
— Plan trips throughout Russia and Ukraine;
— Get voice turn-by-turn directions;
— Use voice commands (in Russian);
— Recalculate routes based on the latest traffic information;
— Search for locations by name or addresses, as well as businesses by name or category;
— Receive warnings about road works accidents etc. and speed cameras;
— Save addresses to your Favorites and plan routes to them quickly;
— Get additional information about road congestion levels en route;
— Check the indicator for traffic levels;
— Browse vector maps for Moscow, the Moscow Region, Saint Petersburg and Ukraine.

镇附近找到自己的方式，只用最快的路线和清晰的道路或整个国家。这个应用程序提供转由俄罗斯和乌克兰的转动方向，并可以安排行程，以目前的交通条件下，封闭等方面综合考虑。

随着Yandex.Navigator应用程序中，您可以：
- 自动更新每月的路线图;
- 访问总是在线地图到您的设备上;
- 计划行程遍及俄罗斯和乌克兰;
- 获取语音转由转动方向;
- 使用语音命令（俄罗斯）;
- 最新的交通信息的基础上重新计算路由;
- 搜索地点的名称或地址，以及企业名称或类别;
- 接收道路工程事故等高速摄像机的警告;
- 保存地址到您的收藏以及计划路线，他们迅速;
- 获取更多信息途中的道路拥堵水平;
- 检查流量水平的指标;
- 浏览莫斯科，莫斯科州，圣彼得堡和乌克兰的矢量地图。
