说明
A live wallpaper which displays a grid of lights fading in and out. Completely customize the shapes, colors, and speeds!

Features:
+ Squares, circles, hexagons, and more!
+ Save and restore themes!
+ Share your settings with others over email, Twitter, and more!
+ Many interactive touch options!
+ Animated ripple and wave effects!
+ Randomly generate brand new wallpapers!

To add a Settings shortcut to your homescreen, press Home > Menu > Add > Widgets > Light Grid Pro.

Want to use pictures for your background? Check out our newest creation, Photile Live Wallpaper - it's Light Grid with pictures!

For Halloween, football, and many more icons, grab our Holiday Themes! Please note: The Holiday Theme pack is a separate $0.99 purchase from the Pro version, so that the user may decide whether to buy Pro, icons, or both.

SD Card permission is needed for saving screenshots.

Problems? Email below and we'll help you out!

"Move to SD card" is now enabled on Froyo (2.2) and up. Please note that if you use this feature for any live wallpaper, it will be disabled if you reboot or unmount your SD card. In this case, you will have to reselect Light Grid Pro for your live wallpaper.
